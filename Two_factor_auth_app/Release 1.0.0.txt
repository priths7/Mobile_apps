Dependencies:
com.google.firebase:firebase-auth:19.3.0

minSdkVersion: 16
targetSdkVersion 29

Android Studio Version: 3.6.2

Features:

1. Animated UI Background, the background colour changes in a certain duration time.

2. Login and Signup to your account to get started.

3. Two factor authentication: Using the google firebase authentication, a 2 factor authentication has been implemented. 
Enter your login details ( Emailid , password) to proceed to otp authentication. Enter your mobile no, 
to proceed with the login process.

4. Shows your Email ID and UID(Unique Identification) code in the database.
